Meet Assemblyman Erik Dilan

Erik Martin Dilan has dedicated more than two decades to public service in Brooklyn—fighting for safer streets, stronger tenant protections, affordable housing, and resources that strengthen working families.

  • ✔ Longtime advocate for affordable housing
  • ✔ Champion for public safety and community investment
  • ✔ Record of delivering resources to schools and senior centers
  • ✔ Former NYC Council Member & experienced legislator
  • ✔ Lifelong North Brooklyn resident and product of NYC public schools
  • ✔ Serving in the State Assembly since 2015, representing the 54th District
  • ✔ Chair of the Assembly Committee on Correction and member of key committees on housing, energy, insurance, and the budget

Fighting for Affordable Housing

Erik has been at the forefront of legislative efforts to protect tenants, expand rent stabilization, and fund affordable housing construction. His record shows a commitment to keeping New Yorkers housed with dignity and stability.

Building Safer, Stronger Neighborhoods

From public safety investments to violence interruption programs, Erik brings an experienced, balanced approach to keeping families safe while strengthening community trust and accountability.

Supporting Working Families

Erik continues to champion legislation expanding childcare access, worker protections, and cost-of-living relief—because every family deserves a fair shot.
