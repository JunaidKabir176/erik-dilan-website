Volunteer with Team Erik

Campaigns are won by neighbors talking to neighbors. Whether you have a few hours a month or time every week, there’s a way to plug in.

  • Knock on doors and talk with voters in your neighborhood.
  • Make calls or send texts to remind supporters about key dates.
  • Help staff tables at community events, forums, and block parties.
  • Support office tasks like data entry, mailings, and logistics.

Use the form on the left to tell us how you’d like to help. A member of the volunteer team will follow up with next steps and upcoming opportunities.

Prefer email? Reach us at info@erikdilancampaign.org and include your name, neighborhood, and how you’d like to get involved.
